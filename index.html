Tooth and Paw is a brand new concept in canine wear. We believe that dog apparel should not only be stylish, but also useful. That's why Tooth and Paw jackets are made with the finest materials and construction- but keep functionality at the core.

Each Tooth and Paw original is made right here in the USA and is customizable to your furry friend. Since each dog is unique, we let the customer choose the right features and look for them!

At left, Charlie the Terrier is modeling the very first Tooth and Paw prototype for smaller dogs, which is water-resistant and includes a carry handle for lifting smaller dogs without hurting their throats.

Tooth and Paw jackets may feature:

  • Water-proof or water-resistant outer shells
  • Soft luxurious inner lining
  • Reflective safety detailing for evening walks
  • Functional pockets for treats and doggie bags
  • Build your own! Select your own fabrics, trims, and options to customize to your dog
  • Limited edition fabrics. We constantly update our choices so you get a unique product
  • Removable GPS tracker currently under testing! Peace of mind of always being able to locate your dog using your smartphone
  • Laser cut personalized tags with name and owner contact phone number
  • Leash attachments on top or front, depending on preference
  • Carry handles for ease of lifting and control. Recommended for smaller dogs
  • Unique details- like detachable hoods with optional ear slits
  • Satisfaction guarantee! Free shipping in the USA

FAQ

How do I redeem my reward?
Please email your order ID to hello@toothandpaw.com after ordering via Bitcoin. We will respond with more information regarding your reward. If you opted to pre-order a jacket, you will be able to pick your design/options/fabrics at this point.
Do you work with any dog shelters or non-profits?
Yes! We are dedicated to helping less fortunate dogs find happy homes and stay healthy. We donate 10% of all profits to organizations furthering animal welfare and are also working on forming local dog shelter partnerships. Any excess inventory is donated to dogs in need at various shelters.
