The Magic Hack Bus!

Where hackathon meets fun new experiences.

As busy engineers, how better to optimize than by using otherwise wasted transport time to solve problems and create with other makers?

At the end of your wi-fi enabled bus ride hack session, you will arrive at a fun locale where you can-

  • Hike on coastal bluffs and commune with the local elk in Point Reyes
  • Learn how to rock climb in Pinnacles National Park
  • Debate the tannin profile of Pinot Noirs versus Syrahs in Napa Valley
  • Grab first chair and fresh powder at one of our world-reknowned Tahoe mountains
  • And much more! Our trips go as far as your imagination...

Special Topic Immersions

In addition to our shorter duration trips, which last from 1 day to 1 weekend, The Magic Hack Bus transports you to destination hack retreats lasting from 1 to 2 weeks. Each hack retreat focuses on a particular topic, language, or skill and draws on the expertise of leading industry engineers. An immersive 1 to 2 week experience is the perfect way to bring your coding to the next level, while providing needed downtime in the outdoors to recharge and ponder the big things in life.

Match Score

Community and expanding social and professional circles is a big part of The Magic Hack Bus mission. Therefore, we provide match scores between you and all other participants for each trip so that you know ahead of time whether you are likely to click!

How do we do this? By using our heuristic score system that finds commonalities between you and other participants such as schools, friends in common, dev languages, interests, and hobbies.

Commitment to Giving Back

The Magic Hack Bus proudly supports the next generation of tech creators. That is why 10% of the profits from each trip you take with us goes to send a bright young elementary school student from a disadvantaged neighborhood to summer code camp.

FAQ

How do you decide where to go?
We ask you to tell us! Vote on our website and if we get enough votes, we'll run a trip.
What if I don't feel like coding on the way back after an event?
No problem! There are times to create and times to chill out. Our bus is equipped with relaxing and lounging areas if you are not feeling up to hacking. We also offer a range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages and snacks at our mobile bar.
